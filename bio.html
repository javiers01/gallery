About Me

JavierS Maza is an executive Producer at Zinkvision, where he leads the production of high-quality animation for series, films, and Web3 projects. With a successful track record deliver animation services for 200% Wolf, Blippi Wonders, Hot Wheels and Disney cruise interactive.


Previously, he collaborated with top studios like Warner Brothers, Sony, Nickelodeon Pictures, and some major brands such as Rio, Penguins of Madagascar, and Puss in Boots. His background in storytelling and as a top creative producer at a commercial houses, combined with his expertise in technology and business development, positions him as a leading force in animation innovation.
